01 Valuation Methodology Selection

RegMind is, in substance, a pre-revenue pre-seed asset: one signed pilot that has not begun billing, one unsigned prospect, a single small jurisdiction, and no production customer. At this stage valuation is anchored on team, IP and market rather than on financials — no single method is decisive, and any method applied to a USD 60K base produces a wide range. A blended approach is used. Because the asset is IP/product-led, the build-vs-buy / IP anchor carries the most weight and the thin ARR multiple the least; the reverse weighting (Rev. 3) under-valued the asset.

04 Build-vs-Buy / Differentiated-IP Anchor

This is the primary anchor for an IP-led asset. It values the components an acquirer would actually retain, and credits the calendar time a buyer skips by acquiring rather than building. The commodity layers — generic CRUD/auth/RBAC, single-file frontends, basic infra — would be re-platformed by any buyer and add little, so they are excluded. What is genuinely hard to reconstruct is the encoded compliance domain expertise, the deterministic pipeline design, and the ~6,000-test regression corpus, which together represent an 18–30 month, USD 2.5–4M build for a competent team starting from scratch.

Build-vs-buy anchor: ≈ USD 1.5M (net). Below this, the asset is mispriced relative to the cost and time of reconstructing its differentiated IP. Value sits overwhelmingly in the encoded compliance IP and pipeline design — not in infrastructure, and not yet in commercial traction. Attribution percentages are of the differentiated value, not the headline figure.

Floor condition (USD 1.3M)

Pilot 2 does not sign (or is also related-party), and/or IP-assignment gaps surface; valued near the net build-vs-buy IP value with founder strength as the main support. Below this the asset is mispriced relative to its IP + team.

Ceiling condition (USD 2.7M)

Clean IP assignment confirmed, a genuinely third-party Pilot 2 signed, and either SOC 2 Type I underway or a second-jurisdiction configuration in progress. Beyond this (into strategic-acquirer territory) requires a regulator-validated independent production customer (see §07).

Together these move the estimate from ~USD 1.9M toward the USD 2.7M ceiling (and into strategic-acquirer territory) with little or no capital deployment.
