## 1. Application review checklist

### Identity and profile

- [ ] Application belongs to the correct client and application ID/ref.
- [ ] Legal name, registration number, country, sector, entity type, and contact details are coherent.
- [ ] Application status and review route match the work being performed.
- [ ] Officer of record is assigned; any non-owner action is documented.
- [ ] Material changes or inconsistencies are recorded and escalated.

### Corporate structure and persons

- [ ] Directors reviewed.
- [ ] UBOs/controllers reviewed.
- [ ] Intermediaries and relevant representatives reviewed.
- [ ] Ownership structure is understandable and supported by evidence.
- [ ] Complex, opaque, nominee, or unusual ownership is escalated.

### Documents and verification

- [ ] Required corporate documents are present.
- [ ] Required director/UBO/controller documents are present.
- [ ] Documents are attributable to the correct application/person.
- [ ] Document verification and IDV status are current.
- [ ] Rejected, expired, missing, or unverifiable documents have a recorded follow-up.

### Screening, risk, and memo

- [ ] Screening status and provider mode were checked.
- [ ] Potential sanctions, PEP, and adverse-media matches were reviewed.
- [ ] Risk score, risk level, dimensions, and provenance were reviewed.
- [ ] Risk is current; stale or quarantined risk is treated as a stop condition.
- [ ] Compliance memo is present, current, validated, and consistent with evidence.
- [ ] Supervisor result, warnings, contradictions, and recommendations were reviewed.
- [ ] `INCONSISTENT` or unexplained output was escalated.

### Open controls and auditability

- [ ] Enhanced requirements and critical blockers were checked.
- [ ] RMI/document requests are resolved or their status is documented.
- [ ] Periodic-review or monitoring obligations were checked where in scope.
- [ ] Activity/audit log was reviewed for relevant actions and exceptions.
- [ ] Decision records and sign-off requirements are present where applicable.
- [ ] Evidence export was created when policy requires it.

## 2. Pre-approval checklist

- [ ] No unresolved critical blocker remains.
- [ ] Risk is current and its provenance is acceptable.
- [ ] Memo is current, validated, and approved where required.
- [ ] Screening is current and terminal status is understood.
- [ ] Required IDV/document verification is complete or an approved exception exists.
- [ ] Evidence pack/export is available when required.
- [ ] Any escalation or override has a documented reason and authorized reviewer.
- [ ] Required four-eyes or senior review has been completed by a distinct authorized officer.
- [ ] The proposed route is permitted for the officer's role and the case risk.
- [ ] Backend approval gates permit the proposed action.

## 3. Final approval checklist

- [ ] Final status matches the evidence and approved route.
- [ ] Approval rationale is specific and recorded.
- [ ] No stale or quarantined risk remains.
- [ ] No stale or unapproved memo remains.
- [ ] No unresolved RMI or critical enhanced requirement remains.
- [ ] No unresolved screening hit requiring review remains.
- [ ] No unsupported AI-only or supervisor-only conclusion is being used.
- [ ] Required dual-control/four-eyes review is complete.
- [ ] Officer sign-off is complete.
- [ ] Backend decision gate returned success.
- [ ] Audit/activity and decision records are present after the action.

- [ ] Backend rejection gate returned success.

## 5. Periodic Review checklist

- [ ] Correct application, review cycle, and named owner confirmed.
- [ ] Client attestation reviewed.
- [ ] Material changes in ownership, directors, UBOs, business, geography, products, or risk factors checked.
- [ ] New or changed documents are present and verified.
- [ ] Screening and adverse-media status reviewed.
- [ ] Risk reassessment is current and supported by provenance.
- [ ] Memo/addendum reviewed where required.
- [ ] RMI and enhanced requirements are resolved or escalated.
- [ ] Open completion blockers are resolved.
- [ ] Completion rationale and next review date recorded.

## 6. Monitoring and alerts checklist (only if explicitly in pilot scope)

- [ ] Management has confirmed monitoring is in pilot scope.
- [ ] Alert belongs to the correct application/client.
- [ ] Source, severity, evidence, and status were reviewed.
- [ ] Potential sanctions, PEP, adverse media, or suspicious activity was escalated as required.
- [ ] Dismissal/closure rationale and authorized reviewer were recorded.
- [ ] Any excluded monitoring capability is not being represented as active.

If monitoring is not explicitly enabled, mark this section **excluded** and do not treat an empty queue as evidence of no risk.
