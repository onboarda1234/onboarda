Risk Scoring Model Configuration

Manage the 5 risk dimensions, their weights, sub-criteria, and classification thresholds. Changes apply to all new applications.

Classification Thresholds

Composite score boundaries that determine the risk level. Score is 0–100.

Country Risk Classifications

Countries are scored based on FATF and sanctions lists. Score mapping: FATF Black = 4, Sanctioned = 4, FATF Grey = 3, Standard = 2, Low-Risk = 1.

Business Sector Risk Scores

Each business sector is assigned a risk score from 1 (low) to 4 (very high).

Entity Type Risk Scores

Each entity type is assigned a risk score from 1 (low) to 4 (very high).

Low Risk: 0 — 29.9
Medium Risk: 30 — 49.9
High Risk: 50 — 69.9

Approval Requirements
All applications: Require compliance memo (generated, validated, supervisor-approved), live screening, and no flagged documents
Low / Medium Risk: Single officer approval (Admin, SCO, or CO)
High / Very High Risk: Dual approval by two different officers + pre-approval before KYC
All approvals are human-initiated. No auto-approval exists regardless of risk score.
